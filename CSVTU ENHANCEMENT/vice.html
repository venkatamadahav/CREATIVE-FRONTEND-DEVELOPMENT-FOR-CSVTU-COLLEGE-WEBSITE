Prof. Dr. M. K. Verma
Vice Chancellor

Profile Web Page: Click Here
Phone (Office of PS):

Appreciating the growing need in the modern technical era, the State Government established a separate Technical University in the year 2005. Since then the University has been trying its utmost to take it to newer heights. Despite several challenges and infrastructural constraints, the University has created a new avenue for technical education and has made sustained efforts to establish its identity at the national level It has always emphasized on its qualitative standards. Proper strategies, Continuous monitoring and technical guidance, coupled with the cooperation of the affiliated institutions have contributed towards achieving the present status of the university.

The University is trying to gain the confidence of society, by way of improving the standard of teaching and by maintenance of the university calendar. Digital valuation system was introduced for speedy preparation of results. The University is also updating the syllabus as per the requirements of a competitive world. Research and development activities are the parameters of academic excellence. A significant number of research papers have been published by the college teachers in the national and international journals.

The affiliated colleges also organizes a large number of national and international seminars, workshops on specific areas in addition to Industry Institute Interaction on Emerging Technologies etc. With a view to improve the quality of teaching, the University organizes training program and thirteen workshops to train the teachers and for quality development of the students, where experts from different streams were invited from outside. The colleges were also persuaded to conduct a large number of such trainings in their institutions. Academic inspection of all the affiliated colleges are carried out.

The University celebrates Yuva Utsava on Swami Vivekanand’s birthday. Sports tournament and cultural activities are also organized by the University in various affiliated colleges.

The University NSS units actively participates in various social activities. Due to various achievements, the University bagged the Emerging Technological University of the year Award from the World Management Congress. I sincerely wish that in near future, the University will not only reach dizzying heights in the fields of technical education but also meet the hopes and aspirations of society.

(Dr. M. K. Verma)
